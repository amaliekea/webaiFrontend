Help and Documentation

How to Use StudyHelper

  1. Enter a topic or question in the first text area (e.g., “Explain Docker”).
  2. Specify the difficulty level (e.g., easy, medium, hard).
  3. Choose whether to include a quiz by typing “true” or “false”.
  4. Click Generate to receive an AI-generated explanation and optional quiz.

Managing Subjects

In the right panel, you see the different study subjects weprovide. This helps keep your learning organized.

FAQ

Q: What if nothing happens when I click "Generate"?
A: Make sure all fields are filled in and that you have internet connection. Try refreshing the page.

Q: What does the difficulty level do?
A: It helps the AI generate an explanation that matches your level of understanding.
